Personal Profile

I am a Full Stack Developer with a passion for JavaScript.

I am a JavaScript developer with a passion for writing clean, structured code. I am organized, motivated and work well by myself or in a group. I love learning new technologies and improving my existing skills.

Over 15 years ago, I decided to become real estate investor. While working a full time job, I educated myself and started pursuing that goal. Within 5 years I had built the business up to a point where I could leave my job and pursue investing full time.

About 5 years ago, while creating a website for my business, I discovered that I had a real passion for web development. I started out creating a simple 'Point and Click'​ Drupal website, but found myself needing to know 'how'​ this thing worked under the hood. So I peeked into the source files and it just hit me, this is what I want to do!

I started by learning HTML, CSS, and PHP, then I discovered JavaScript and the myriad of related frameworks. My passion grew with every new technology that I learned and I knew that this was my new career.

After many books, tutorials, and online courses, I felt that if I wanted to make a career change I would need a more structured approach. After much research, I decided upon Udacity. Their hands on, project based courses are well designed and the feedback from the project reviewers is excellent.

The skills I have learned as a small business owner, such as Self-Motivation, Project Management, Problem Solving, and Context Switching, will help me in my transition to a Full Stack Developer. While I continue be a real estate investor, I have outsourced the daily operations in order to pursue my passion.
